Niwee Ecosystem

The NIWEE ecosystem’s purpose is to draw together key actors and stakeholders that gravitate toward developing business ventures and innovation, including entrepreneurs, mentors, incubators, sources of talent such as universities, corporations, investors, technologists and artists and supporting services like startup savvy law and accounting firms.

Why Niwee?

Business women often get discounted because of their gender, their cultural heritage, the colour of their skin, their sexual orientation, for being a Mom or for having too few assets. It often gets worst when it comes to Indigenous women, bias often results in inequities like health, food security, sub-standard housing, fatigue, under employment, very little access to networks. Like so many other women, they must face systematic barriers to achieving their dream of launching and growing a business of their choice. Let’s talk truth there are no countries in the world with gender equality.
